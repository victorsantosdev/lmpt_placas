	This directories contain the source and project files for the necessary libraries
used in the data acquision systems Sensu and Climus. For its utilization in third part
applications, follow these steps:

	In the C++ Builder, include the files *.lib (stored in the ./include directory)
in your project;

	The third part application must be access the files *.dll (stored in the ./lib
directory). Therefore, include the directory who contain the *.dll in your path.
